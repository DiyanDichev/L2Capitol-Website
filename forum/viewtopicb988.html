2.1 A player agrees to comply with these regulations and the forum rules.
2.2 In special cases, not covered by these rules a player is obliged to use common sense in evaluating the situation, actions or statements of his and other players.
2.3 The player is obliged to notify the Administration of the server found errors in the functioning of the game. Critical errors allowing for a substantial modification of the game, easy game by cheating must be reported by the Administration of Private Messages (PM) in the forum.

3.1 The administration is always right.
3.2 The administration is not giving away items, adena etc. Any requests for items, Adena may result in punishment.
3.3 The administration does not modify the form of players. Any request for modification, raising / lowering statistics can result in a punishment.
3.4 The administration dont summon to cites mobs/players, not teleport players, players are not transformed, any requests for teleporting, transformation can result in punishment.
3.5 The Administration may summon mobs / players, transform them, or teleport only:
- During the event
- Under review players
- In a situation that requires (eg support to the player at the stck of his character)
- In any situation where it is needed by the Authority to carry out their duties.
3.6 The Administration is using the petition to come into contact with the players. Important information is provided on the channel Annoucements and are written in English.
3.7 The Administration may use their native language or languages acquired in each channel except channel Annoucements, unless this message is a translation of the English version.
